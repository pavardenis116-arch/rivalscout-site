Questions

FAQ

Which pages can you monitor?

Any public page — pricing pages, changelogs, homepages, careers pages. Standard pages work immediately; JavaScript-heavy pages are read with a full browser engine. If a page genuinely can't be read, we tell you rather than pretending.

How is it personalised to my product?

At setup you describe your product in a few sentences. Every report and alert is then written relative to you — price gaps versus your plans, features you do or don't have, positioning overlaps. It's intelligence about your specific situation, not generic market news.

How quickly will I find out about a change?

Pages are checked every morning. A change made today reaches your inbox by tomorrow morning at the latest.

What happens if nothing changes for weeks?

You still get your day-one baseline report immediately, and the Monday digest every week. Quiet weeks get a calm two-liner confirming monitoring is active. Change alerts come whenever your market actually moves.

How do I cancel?

One click from any email we send, or via your Stripe billing portal. No phone calls, no retention flows, no questions.
